The Best Movies According to Don'Vielle

Top 3 Movies of All time

Neon Genesis Evangelion

This movie is an emotional masterpiece, I love the spin on the God's creation of mankind through Genesis and Adam. Beautiful story telling. New spin on the origin's of mankind kind. Very vivid art and highly relateable in terms of sacrafice.

Ghost in a Shell

I love this movie because of how it displays the potentail future of cybernetics and AI. It has very emotional story-telling and ia a crime, sci-fi, action, drama. Definetly worth the watch.

Alita Battle Angel

Again a super emotinal sci-fi film about an ancient cybernetic warrior who defended Earth before what was called 'The Great Fall'. The movie begin's with Alita who is discovered in a cyber-junk wasteland. The movie takes you on a thrilling journey and mysetery to reveal Alita's hidden past. Very heart-pulling story with the perfect amount of sci-fi.
